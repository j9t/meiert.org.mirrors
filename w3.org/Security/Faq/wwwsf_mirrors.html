You may mirror this document by copying and unpacking the following tar archive:
https://www.w3.org/Security/Faq/www-security-faq.tar.gz
You should then set up a cron job to check this site at regular intervals and update your copy. You can use the w3mir program for this purpose. Please send me e-mail if you set up a mirror site in a country that isn't already a mirror sponsor so that I may add you to this list (more sites in South America and Asia are needed!).
